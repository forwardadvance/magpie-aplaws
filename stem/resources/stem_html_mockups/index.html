Stem

Welcome to the STEM website which is designed to keep you informed on what is happening, and being planned in your neighbourhood.

STEM stands for Stronger Together in East Middlesbrough, and serves residents of Park End, Berwick Hills, Netherfields, Priestfields, Pallister Park, Thorntree and Brambles Farm. We are funded through Government until 2010.
