About MGrid

The Middlesbrough Grid (MGrid) for Learning education portal is designed to make information, links and resources available to anyone connected with education in Middlesbrough. As a key priority of Middlesbrough Council it is designed to improve communication and access for all. You are invited to use the feedback button to help in the development of this portal.
